Breed History

The briardwhich is believed to have originated in france,has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep.The breed was used by the French Army as sentries,messenger and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing.Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering.Charlemagne,Napoleon,Thomas Jefferson and Lafayyette all owned briards.
