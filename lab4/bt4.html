The Cottage Garden

the cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses informal design, dense plantings,and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries,although they were re-invented in 1870's English,when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estate gardens

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants,with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs,along with some fruit trees
